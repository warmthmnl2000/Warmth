About Us

WARMTH is a sand candle business that offers handmade products with creativity, customizable designs, a personal touch, and decorative appeal. Our sand-based candles provide unique textures and colors, allowing customers to personalize each piece into a work of art.

Information

We specialize in handcrafted sand candles, each designed with care and attention to detail. Our goal is to provide unique and artistic candles that suit various preferences and spaces.

Vision

To be a leading provider of artistic and customizable sand candles, bringing warmth and creativity into every home.

Mission

Our mission is to craft high-quality sand candles that blend artistry with functionality, offering customers an opportunity to personalize their spaces with beautifully designed candles.
